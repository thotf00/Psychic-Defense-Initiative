# Practical Techniques of Psychic Self-Defense: Murry Hope

## 📖 **Introduction & Core Concepts**
- **Purpose**: Guide for safely navigating psychic/occult development, emphasizing protection and self-defense.
- **Core Premise**: Just as you wouldn’t cross a busy street without looking, you shouldn’t explore consciousness without precautions.

## 👁️ **Chapter 1: The Development of Psychic Gifts**
### Key Definitions:
- **Psychic**: Relates to the soul/psyche; involves tuning into other minds (incarnate/discarnate), the collective unconscious, past/future.
- **Occultist**: Aims to manipulate cosmic forces via mind/will (positive/directive); may not be psychic (e.g., John Dee used mediums).
- **Mystic**: Often religious; observes cosmic energies without directing/receiving; seeks spiritual knowledge.
- **Polarity System**: Positive (occultist) + Negative (psychic/medium) = Best results (e.g., priest/prophet, magus/scryer).

### Cosmic Law & Evil:
- **Cosmic Law**: Ordered pattern of the universe; harmonious when followed.
- **Evil**: Misplaced/misdirected energy operating against cosmic law; selfish/destructive vs. good (selfless/constructive).

### Psychic Awareness & Suitability:
- **Brain Hemispheres**: Left = logic, right = imagery/creativity. Western education favors left, causing imbalance.
- **Aquarian Age Shift**: Reawakens right brain, leading to spiritual exploration but also potential instability.
- **Not for Everyone**: Requires strong, stable personality. Self-assessment is crucial before beginning.

### Recognizing & Interpreting Experiences:
- **Archetypal Symbology**: Subconscious communicates via symbols (tarot, runes, etc.).
- **Inspiration Sources**: Not just “higher self”—connects to universal mind/pool of intelligences.
- **First Steps**: Use creative imagination (e.g., visualize a green field, explore imagery). Encounters (person/animal/symbol) reveal aspects of self or group soul connections.

## ⚖️ **Chapter 2: Occult Laws**
### The Laws:
1. **Law of Challenge**: Place protection, challenge any presence before engaging. Varies by belief system.
2. **Law of Equalities**: In mental/energy battles, the stronger prevails. You cannot defeat a more advanced being with simple symbols (e.g., a crucifix).

### Initiation:
- **Not Formal Exams**: Expansions of consciousness through life experiences, not necessarily “occult” events.
- **Safe Haven**: Ultimately, rely on inner resources and personal understanding of the divine.

## 🌈 **Chapter 3: How to Strengthen the Aura**
- **Aura**: Energy field protecting against disease, mental impingement, energy loss.
- **Damaged Aura**: Leads to illness; shows discoloration/breaks when sick.
- **Control via Thought**: Like learning to ride a bike; use visualization (e.g., stepping into a blue/white “plastic bag” or cloak of light).
- **Colors**: Safe = white, blue, certain greens, pale lilac, gold/silver. Avoid red/brown around head.
- **Masking**: Advanced practitioners can contract aura to avoid drawing attention.

## 🛡️ **Chapter 4: Symbols for Cosmic Protection**
### Universal Safety Symbols:
- **Christian**: Cross, rose, chalice, dove.
- **Egyptian**: Ankh, eye of Horus.
- **Norse/Qabalistic/Tarot/Pantheistic**: Specific runes, sephira, cards, elemental symbols.

### Personal & God Symbols:
- **Personal Symbol**: Unique to you (e.g., tree, hat). Discover via meditation journeying back to conception, knotting a “silver cord” of life events, and rising to see the first image.
- **God Symbol**: Your link to the divine source. Visualize personal symbol, rotate it clockwise, and meet your archetype (god/goddess, parental figure).
- **Use Both**: For safe passage in altered states—connects you to group soul and divine source.

### Individual Protection:
- **Working Alone**: Must be disciplined and self-reliant. Use thought processes or protective ritual.
- **Mind vs. Brain**: Dualism—mind/spirit uses brain as tool. Subconscious relates to outer time/imagery.

## 🧠 **Chapter 5: Mind Over Matter**
### Techniques:
- **Visualization for Protection**: Build aura, extend light beam clockwise to cleanse room.
- **If Things Go Wrong**: Return to normal consciousness, earth yourself, close aura, clear room again. Call for higher help if needed.
- **Thought-Forms**: Can be created/dissolved by advanced practitioners; beginners should avoid.

### Dabbling Dangers:
- **Ouija/Planchettes**: Can attract mischievous spirits or generate psycho-kinetic energy; often cause after-effects.
- **Rule**: Always use safety measures when contacting unseen dimensions.

### Protection Through Ritual:
- **Psychological Need**: Ritual satisfies devotional urge, taps into collective faith power.
- **Don’t Mix Systems**: Incompatible rays can cause psychological/issues. Choose one (Qabalah, Greek, Egyptian, Norse, Pantheism, etc.) and stick to it.
- **Eastern Systems Caution**: Passivity may unbalance Westerners.

### Group Protection:
- **Leader Responsibility**: Karmic duty; must protect, earth, and close auras for all participants.
- **Energy Use**: Direct generated power ethically (healing, peace, helping animals/ecology).
- **Earthing Exercises**: Tense/relax body parts from toes to head to regain full consciousness.

## ☠️ **Chapter 6: Psychic Attacks**
### Meditation & Groups:
- **Open Auras**: Can attract influences; leaders must seal auras post-session to prevent mental imbalance.
- **Artificial Stimulants**: Drugs/alcohol damage brain cells, distort perceptions, and are unsafe for psychic work.

### What is a Psychic Attack?
- **Often Misdiagnosed**: Can be mental illness, suppressed emotions, or everyday stress.
- **Genuine Attack**: Mind encounters incompatible/hostile frequency from another intelligence/energy.
- **Law of Equalities Applies**: Battle of minds; victor learns, vanquished adjusts.

### Science & Psychism:
- **Aquarian Age**: Merges metaphysics with science (ESP, ASC, OOBE).
- **Brainwaves**: Beta (awake), Alpha (meditation), Theta (dream/psychic), Delta (deep sleep). Each has a natural use.

## 💭 **Chapter 7: Dreams, Astral Travel, and the Chakras**
### Dreams:
- **Function**: Link between inner/outer time; archetypal communication.
- **Nightmares**: Check normal causes (diet, health) first. Use metaphysical clearing if needed (water, light, symbols).
- **Control in Dreams**: Recognize dream state, dissolve threats with thought.

### Astral Travel:
- **Involuntary (sleep) & Voluntary (conscious)**: Same precautions apply.
- **Protection**: Program awareness, change form mentally if pursued (like Celtic tale of Keridwen & Gwion).
- **Safety**: Don’t dabble alone; train with experienced practitioner.

### Psychic Safety Valves:
- **Case Study**: Husband’s “visions” prevented violence—either psychological defense or archetypal protection (Thoth).

### Chakras:
- **Seven Energy Vortices**: Link subtle energies to physical body via endocrine glands.
- **Kundalini**: Serpent fire at base of spine; ascends via chakras, awakening each.
- **Imbalances**: Cause physical/mental illness (e.g., throat chakra = expression issues, ajna = clairvoyance distortion).
- **Warning**: Do not experiment with chakra healing without proper training.

## 🔮 **Chapter 8: Psychic Work in Practice**
### The “Fireside Occultist”:
- **Theory vs. Practice**: Book knowledge isn’t enough; experience is essential.
- **Ethical Responsibility**: Don’t perform readings/healings without proper skill.

### Psychic Readings:
- **Telepathy vs. Time-Projection**: Many “clairvoyants” simply read sitter’s thoughts.
- **Aids (Tarot, I Ching, etc.)**: Cut out conscious mind but interpretation can be biased.
- **Defense During Readings**:
  - Keep room clean (physically/energetically).
  - Build psychic atmosphere aligned with your belief.
  - Detach emotionally; don’t let clients overpower you.
  - Use a “coverer” for trance mediums—projects protective field, issues Law of Challenge.

## 🏛️ **Chapter 10: The Lodge System**
### Rays & Anti-Rays:
- **Incompatible Systems**: E.g., Saturn (lead) cancels Uranus (uranium). Understand principles, not just symbols.
- **Do Not Mix**: Stick to one system until mastered.

### Occult Ranks:
- **Cannot Be Bought**: Titles (adeptus major, etc.) are meaningless if not earned through true development.
- **Wisdom > Knowledge**: Ability to convey complex ideas simply is the mark of a true master.

### Secret Lodges:
- **Historical Context**: Formed for secrecy/safety during persecution.
- **Modern Challenges**: Time constraints, ego clashes, infiltration by discord.
- **Compatibility**: Members must be on similar rays; old souls harmonize beyond systems.
- **Handshakes/Codes**: Mostly historical; some traditions retain them.

### System Compatibilities:
- **Egyptian**: Works with Norse, not always with Christian.
- **Qabalah**: Compatible with Christianity.
- **Eastern/Western**: Often clash.

## 🌪️ **Chapter 11: Elementals and Thought-Forms**
### Elementals (Devas):
- **Fire (Salamanders)**: Creativity, ardour.
- **Air (Sylphs)**: Intellect, communication.
- **Water (Ondines)**: Emotions, receptivity.
- **Earth (Gnomes)**: Practicality, thrift.
- **Relationship**: Your dominant elemental trait attracts corresponding elemental helper.
- **Respect Them**: Win by love/friendship, not command. Thank and dismiss properly after rituals.

### Thought-Forms:
- **Encoded Energy Pockets**: Created intentionally (magic) or accidentally (strong emotions like jealousy).
- **Accidental Creation**: Negative energy in a group can form an “artificial elemental” that attaches to someone, causing discomfort/bad luck.
- **Dissolving**: Group leader must use thought power/Law of Equalities to return energy to sender (with care).
- **Creating Thought-Forms**: Dangerous, unnecessary. If done, they can persist for centuries if not properly released.
- **Trapped Spirits**: Releasing an elemental (e.g., djinn) can bring gratitude/help.

### Inhibiting Phenomena:
- **Raise Vibration**: Use mind power to create a “fast” frequency that inhibits manifestation.
- **Politely Ask Spirits to Refrain**: As with haunted house example.

### Dismissals & Vacuums:
- **Always Place or Dismiss Energies**: Unused energy attaches to the most psychic person, causing problems.
- **Never Leave a Vacuum**: After clearing unwanted vibes, fill space with harmonious energy/color (e.g., blues/greens for calm, gold for energy).
