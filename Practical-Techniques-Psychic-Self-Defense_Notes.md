# Psychic Self-Defense: Techniques and Safety, Murry Hope

The psychic/occult realm is a **real, interactive dimension** with inherent risks. Engaging without protection is likened to “rushing across a busy street without looking.” Dangers range from subtle energetic intrusions to full psychic attacks, and not everyone is suited for this work.

## 🛡️ **Key Principles of Defense**
1. **Aura Control & Strengthening**  
   - The aura is an energy field that protects against disease, mental intrusion, and energy loss.  
   - It can be strengthened and controlled via visualization (e.g., surrounding oneself with blue/white light or a “cloak”).  
   - Advanced practitioners can “mask” their aura to avoid detection.
2. **Symbolic & Ritual Protection**  
   - Use of **personal symbols** (unique to the individual) and **god symbols** (connection to the divine) for safe passage in altered states.  
   - Rituals and prayers from one’s belief system can create a protective “thought force.”  
   - **Never mix incompatible systems** (e.g., Egyptian and Christian rituals)—can cause psychological or energetic conflict.
3. **Mental Discipline & Visualization**  
   - Thought precedes action in metaphysical realms.  
   - Visualization techniques can cleanse spaces, create protective barriers, and dissolve unwanted energies.  
   - **Mind over matter** is real: focused intention can influence energy and matter.
4. **Law of Challenge & Equalities**  
   - **Challenge** any entity or energy before engagement.  
   - **Equalities**: In any conflict, the stronger mind/energy prevails. You cannot defeat a more advanced being with simple tools (e.g., a crucifix).

## ☠️ **Primary Threats & Vulnerabilities**
- **Psychic Attacks**: Often manifest as persistent bad luck, health issues, mental fog, or emotional manipulation.  
- **Misguided or Malicious Entities**: Including “evil” energies (selfish/destructive), thought-forms, elemental spirits, and discarnate beings.  
- **Open or Damaged Auras**: Leave one susceptible to intrusion, “auric limpets,” or psychic “links.”  
- **Mental Instability**: Those with unresolved trauma, mental illness, or weak psychological boundaries are at high risk.  
- **Dabbling Without Knowledge**: Using Ouija boards, drugs, or unstructured meditation can invite unwanted influences.

## ✅ **Safety Protocols**
1. **Before Any Psychic Work**:  
   - Strengthen aura, invoke protective symbols, cleanse the space with light.  
   - Ensure mental and physical health: avoid drugs, alcohol, heavy food beforehand.
2. **During Work**:  
   - Stay within your level of experience.  
   - If uncomfortable, **immediately return to normal consciousness**, close your aura, cleanse the area.  
   - Use the Law of Challenge.
3. **After Work**:  
   - Properly “earth” yourself (physical awareness exercises).  
   - Close your aura, seal the space.  
   - Direct any unused energy ethically (e.g., toward healing).
4. **Group Work**:  
   - Leaders must protect all participants, ensure everyone’s aura is closed afterward, and direct group energy responsibly.

## ⚖️ **Ethical & Psychological Foundations**
- **Evil = Selfish/Destructive | Good = Selfless/Constructive**  
- **Self-Honesty & Stability** are prerequisites—know your limits, process trauma before exploring.  
- **Respect All Consciousness**: From humans to animals to elemental spirits.  
- **No Condemnation of Other Systems**: Different paths suit different people; avoid judgment.
